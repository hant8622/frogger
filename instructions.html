Aim
Navigate the frog using the W, A, S, D key and help it cross from the bottom to all of the 5 slots on top.

Avoid the cars, stay afloat on the river by landing on the logs and turtles, and fill the slots.

If players want to MANUALLY reset the game, press the ENTER key.

Players will receive points after filling each EMPTY slot. After filling all 5 slots, objects (cars, logs, and turtles) will speed up and the game continues.

Players only will have ONE life. So make sure to avoid colliding with cars, falling into the river and hitting the game boundaries :)
